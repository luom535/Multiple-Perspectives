Silver Giant

At the center of the sci-fi city is a twisted modern building that resembles a skyscraper in an invisible bubble that appears to be suspended in the air. Next to the building, a tall tower decorated with a vintage clock face stands. Next to it is a huge planet and other celestial bodies, forming a fantasy cosmic landscape. A silver giant stands in the foreground, wearing futuristic equipment, as if exploring the meaning of emotion in the cold world of technology. The sky in the background is a gradient of colors, from purple to orange, and I move through it.
