Temple River

Amidst the clouds and mist, several classical pavilions are suspended in the air. Around the pavilions, several elegant egrets are flying. There was no sunshine, the whole scene was foggy, and there were many beautiful flowers and plants growing there. I smelled the fragrance of flowers mixed with water vapor, and I ran forward. There is a lake ahead, surrounded by a lot of mirrors made of water. There is a temple in the middle of the lake. Memory fragments rained down from the sky, and I saw my memory. The pieces fell to the ground and became trees, and the trees grew into forests. I suddenly fell, and a transparent spirit-like wolf caught me. We disappeared together. . .
